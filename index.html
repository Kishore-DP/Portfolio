Summary

Incoming Electronics & Communication Engineering (VLSI Design) undergraduate and an AI-native builder. I turn ideas into shipped products — web apps, mobile apps, interactive 3D sites and games — by pairing product thinking with AI coding tools like Claude: I design it, direct the build, then deploy and refine it. I own projects end to end, from concept and UI to hosting and iteration, and back it up with hands-on electronics (Arduino / ESP32) that ties into my VLSI focus.

Projects

SiteGen AI In DevNext.js · React · AI SaaS

An AI platform that turns a single Google Maps link into a fully hosted business website — imports business data via the Google Places API, generates copy and layout with Claude & ChatGPT, then deploys to Vercel / Cloudflare in minutes.

Interactive 3D Portfolio LiveThree.js · WebGL · PWA

A walkable "medieval market" portfolio built on Three.js — BVH collision, draw-call batching and GPU-adaptive quality hold 60fps even on integrated graphics. Installable offline PWA (this document).
